# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

Follow these 8 actionable tips to protect your digital and financial identity.

1. [cite_start]**Strong Passwords:** Use complex, unique passwords for every account; avoid using birthdates or common names[cite: 1].
2. [cite_start]**Mandatory 2FA:** Enable Two-Factor Authentication on all sensitive accounts, including email and social media[cite: 1].
3. [cite_start]**Phishing Awareness:** Critically evaluate all emails and messages; avoid clicking links that create a false sense of urgency[cite: 1].
4. [cite_start]**Verify Before Trusting:** If an offer seems "too good to be true," verify it through official company websites or trusted sources[cite: 1].
5. **Secure Your Devices:** Ensure your laptop and mobile phone have the latest security patches and OS updates installed.
6. **Digital Hygiene:** Regularly review app permissions and delete unused accounts to reduce your attack surface.
7. **UPI Safety (PIN Rule):** Remember: You **NEVER** need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
8. **Verify Recipient:** Always double-check the recipient's name on the confirmation screen before hitting the "Pay" button.
