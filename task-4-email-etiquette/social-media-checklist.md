# Social Media Etiquette: Student Guide

As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have compiled this checklist to help peers maintain a professional and safe social media presence.

## ✅ The Do's
* **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Always add an extra layer of security to your social media accounts.
* **Verify Information:** Always double-check news or claims from trusted sources before sharing or reposting content.
* **Maintain Professionalism:** Keep your public profiles clean and appropriate, as potential employers and recruiters often review them.
* **Build Your Brand:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to showcase your academic achievements and technical projects.
* **Use Professional Emails:** Sign up for professional platforms using your college or a professional-sounding email address.

## ❌ The Don'ts
* **Avoid Oversharing:** Never post sensitive personal information like your home address, phone number, or government IDs publicly.
* **Mind Your Tone:** Do not use ALL CAPS, excessive emojis, or slang when communicating in a formal or professional digital space.
* **Ignore Suspicious Links:** Never click on links sent via direct messages from unknown or unverified accounts.
* **Reactive Posting:** Do not post content when you are angry or emotional; remember that your digital footprint is permanent.
* **Neglect Privacy Settings:** Do not leave your accounts on "Public" by default; regularly audit your privacy and data sharing settings.
